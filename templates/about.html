About Us

The Mathematics Society is an undergradaute community of quantitatively-minded students at CUNY Baruch College dedicated to the exploration of mathematics and the use of mathematics in the fields where it is applicable—in today's world, everywhere. We host presentations and study groups for and on topics ranging from number theory to modern physics to financial engineering, representing as best we can the broad variety of fields in which math is useful, even required, today.

For more information on our events visit our Calendar.
